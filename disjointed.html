disjointed (2020)

A film about the experience of two men working in the industrial food complex.

The film deals with a patriarchal paradigm in crisis and offers a performative space to relive and heal from trauma, where work movements become a way of embodying what remains inaccessible through words and descriptions.

disjointed challenges ideas about an organic and traditional nature of our food, highlighting the artificiality of a reality rarely seen unmediated.
